# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free project for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or viewpoint.

## Expected behaviour

Project participants should:

- communicate with empathy, patience, and technical clarity;
- give constructive feedback focused on the work rather than the person;
- respect differing experiences and accept responsibility for mistakes;
- protect private information and avoid sharing another person's details without permission;
- help keep issues, pull requests, and discussions relevant and productive.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, threats, deliberate intimidation, sexualized content, sustained disruption, and publishing private information are not acceptable in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions that violate these standards and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented privately, and handled consistently.

Report conduct concerns privately through the contact options on [Fatemeh Sabourinia's GitHub profile](https://github.com/fatemehsabourinia). Do not include sensitive personal information in a public issue. Reports will be reviewed in good faith, with privacy respected as far as reasonably possible.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to repository issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, and other project-related interactions where someone represents the project.

This policy is informed by the principles of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), adapted into a concise project-specific form.
